Day 3 — Learning Role-Based Prompting 🚀

What I Learned Today

Today I learned about Role-Based Prompting and how assigning AI a specific role can dramatically improve the quality of responses.

Role-Based Prompting means telling AI to act like a specific expert before asking a question.
This gives the AI context, perspective, expertise, and a clearer direction for the response.

I understood that:

- Better roles create better outputs
- AI responses become more relevant and actionable
- Context changes the depth and quality of answers
- AI can think differently based on the role assigned
