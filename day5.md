Day 5 - Prompt Engineering: The Power of Context

What I Learned

Today I learned why providing context in a prompt is one of the most important parts of prompt engineering.

I compared two prompts:

Prompt A (Without Context)

A simple request:

«Create a 30-day learning roadmap.»

The AI generated a generic roadmap with broad tasks, general resources, and no personalization.

Prompt B (With Context)

A detailed request including:

- Current situation
- Existing skills
- Target goal
- Available time
- Learning style

The AI generated a personalized roadmap with:

- Specific daily tasks
- Relevant resources
- Real projects
- Weekly milestones
- Clear outcomes

Key Insights

- Better prompts produce better results.
- Context helps AI understand the user's actual needs.
- Personalized roadmaps are more actionable and easier to follow.
- Small details can dramatically improve AI-generated outputs.

Activity Completed

- Studied the difference between generic and context-rich prompts.
- Analyzed how context affects AI responses.
- Compared roadmap quality, relevance, and actionability.
- Learned the importance of providing clear goals and constraints.

My Takeaway

The biggest lesson from today is:

«"AI is only as good as the information you provide."»

Adding context transforms AI from giving generic answers to delivering personalized and practical solutions.

Outcome

I now understand how to structure prompts with context to get higher-quality results and more useful responses from AI tools.
